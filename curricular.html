Hatmancers:World Wizarding Finals 20XX

A local multiplayer third-person shooter game developed using Unity. As a wizard, pick up hats and duel against other players using hat magic to win.
Implemented player movement using Unity and C#. Designed game-play mechanics for hats, and created the level in Unity using different 3D models made in MagicaVoxel. Created the shooting animation using Blender

Escape from Hell


A paper based multiplayer board game where the goal is to reach the end gate with the twist being, either everyone wins, or everyone loses. Start as humans and avoid getting possessed by the demon. Use rotatable tiles to your advantage to dispossess the possessed (if any) and escape together.
Responsible for designing the mechanics/rule sheet of the game.

Unity ECS Space
Simulation

ECS Space Simulation is an attempt at exploiting the Data-Oriented approach of Unity Engine. It consists of a rotating Sun and moving asteroids, implemented using the Entity-Component-System of Unity.
sUnity is coming up with a Data-Oriented development approach as an improvement over their traditional Object-Oriented development in terms of performance. The simulation contained about a 1000 asteroid objects and still provides a 60+ FPS

Text Adventure


A simple text based game where the player has to traverse through rooms, collect all resources, and then move into the final Demon room to win and rescue a friend. Not collecting enough resources and entering the Demon room results in death.
The project made me understand some fundamental concepts as well as Data-driven approach in OOP using C#.

Starry Sky

A world succumbed to darkness. What if something other than consciousness, controlled our state of mind? Have you been a part of the wrong side to know if it is really wrong? In Starry Sky, be the ultimate sword master and bring back daylight, by exterminating the creatures of the dark. That is.....until you realise you're becoming one yourself!!!
A completely design-oriented project with the end product being a Game Design Document. I was more involved with Gameplay mechanics, Gameplay progression and the Level design of the first zone in this game. The overall process really helped me realise the kind of questions we need to answer, right from small details in the game itself to how a player playing the game would reciprocate.
